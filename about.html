About

The goal of this website?

Shiny hunting is one of the most rewarding and most frustrating things you can do in a Pokemon game. The problem has never been a lack of information, it's that the information is scattered everywhere. Reddit threads, YouTube videos, wiki pages, fan sites, and Discord servers all have pieces of the puzzle, but none of them have the whole picture in one place.

The Shiny Hunting Guide was built to fix that. One site, every method, every game, no ads. Whether you are picking up shiny hunting for the first time or you have been at it for years and just need a quick odds reference, this site should have what you need.
